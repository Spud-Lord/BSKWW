BBC BITESIZE - FINANCE SECTION
These are examples of where these key words are used.
  • Profit - The total money at the closing balance only if its higher than the opening balance.
  • Loss - The total money at the closing balance only if its lower than the closing balance.
  • Revenue - Sales, bank loans
  • Expenditure - Raw Materials, Utility bills
  • Cash Inflow - Sales, bank loans
  • Cash Outflow - Raw Materials, Utility bills
  • Cashflow -
  • Closing Balance - The money at the end of a specified time.
  • Opening Balance - The money at the start of a specified time.
  • Net Profit - This is the money BEFORE any money is taken away.
  • Gross Profit - This is the money AFTER all money is taken away.
  • Sales - The money from selling your product or service.
  • Fixed Costs - Rent
  • Variable Costs - Raw Materials
  • Break-Even Point - You haven't made a profit or a loss.
  • Margin of Safety - Making a profit but not high enough to match what we want to sell.
